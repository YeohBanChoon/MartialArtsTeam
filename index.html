Problem Statement

#1 Poor Accessibility to Martial Arts

Little to none martial arts schools in vicinity
Costly training fees – financial difficulties to the poor
Occurrence of training errors

#2 Overexertion and bad form

One in five people injured while exercising due to incorrect forms
Tennis elbow, injuries to knee tendons

#3 Lack of Physical Activities

25.1% of Malaysians were physically inactive, contributing to a mortality rate of 16.4% in the country
Increased rate of non-communicable diseases, mortality and decline in physical health

#4 Poor Mental Health

One in every eight people in the world suffers from mental disorders
Leads to mental disorders such as depression and anxiety

Solutions to The Problems

Poor Accessibility to Martial Arts

Provide a way for people to learn martial arts with a lower cost than attending traditional martial arts classes – eradicate cost constraint

Eliminate the time and class location constraint – learn martial arts anytime and anywhere

Occurrence of Training Errors

Provide personalized training volume recommendation – avoid overexertion

Employ AI to detect and analyse motion as well as providing individualized feedback and assistance – avoid bad forms

Lack of Physical Activities

Improve people’s participation in physical activities by fun and interactive way of learning martial arts

Practice martial arts which involve full body engagement

Poor Mental Health

Practice martial arts facilitates the learning of emotional self-control as well as training one’s mind

Learning martial arts as a self-defence method provides a sense of security

Physical exercises like that of martial arts relieve stress and make people feel happier

Reaching out to people with similar interests – feel socially connected

Business Strategy & Commercialisation Status of Product

Market Analysis

Target Audience

Martial arts enthusiasts, practitioners, beginners, schools, instructors and fitness enthusiasts interested in martial arts training

Competitor Analysis

There are apps that provide 3D model demonstration, but as far as we know there is not an app that provide AR model demonstration and AI pose detection and correction features as ours

Monetization Strategy

In-app Advertisement & Subscription Model

Implement a tiered subscription-based pricing model offering monthly and annual subscription options with access to premium content, advanced features, exclusive tutorials, and personalized coaching

Licensing Partnerships

Collaborate with martial art schools, organizations, instructors, brands, and influencers to enhance app offerings, credibility, and monetization potential

Product Development

Content Creation

Collaborate with martial arts experts, instructors, schools, and organizations to create comprehensive, structured, and engaging content tailored to different martial arts styles, techniques, levels, and user preferences

User Experience

Focus on designing an intuitive, user-friendly interface, seamless navigation, interactive learning modules, progress tracking, gamification elements, and customization options to enhance user engagement and satisfaction.
